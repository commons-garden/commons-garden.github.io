Invite-driven onboarding allows users to point to objects and share them with their friends inside or outside SSB platform.

Possible objects:

  • post
  • profile page
  • tag page *
  • user collection *
  • private group
  • private group with automatic invitation (expires after 1w)

* = future state

Used on Manyverse
